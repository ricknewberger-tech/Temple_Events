Getting Started

Sign in with your member access code

1
Enter your access code

On the home page, type in your personal 6-digit access code and tap Sign In.

2
Stay signed in

Check "Remember me on this device" and you won't need to sign in again on the same phone or browser.

📅

Browsing & RSVPing to Events

See what's coming up and save your spot

1
Browse upcoming events

The home page shows all upcoming events. Tap any event card to see full details.

2
RSVP or show interest

Upcoming events have an RSVP button — tap it to reserve your spot. Interested events are still being planned — tap to let the host know you're interested.

3
Add to your calendar

After RSVPing, tap Add to Calendar to save the event to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook.

✨

Creating an Event

Any member can propose or host an event

1
Tap "+ New Event"

You'll find this button at the top of the Upcoming Events page after signing in.

2
Choose the event type

Interested — Post the idea first to gauge interest before committing to a date or location.
Upcoming — Everything is set and members can RSVP right away.

3
Fill in the details

Add a date, time, location, description, and optionally a max attendee count. The more detail you provide, the more excited people will be!

4
Tap "Create Event"

Your event appears on the home page immediately. Everyone in the club can see it and respond.

Tip: Start with an Interested event to see who's in before locking in a venue — then upgrade it to Upcoming once the details are set.
💬

Communicating About Your Event

Keep attendees informed and the conversation going

📢
Post an Update

As the event host, tap Post Update on the event page to share news — changed location, what to bring, parking tips, anything.

💬
Discussion Tab

Any member can ask questions or chat in the Discussion section of each event. Great for coordinating carpools or sharing excitement!

📧
Email All Attendees

Tap Email Attendees on the event page to open a pre-addressed email to everyone who has RSVPed — no copy-paste required.

👥
See Who's Coming

The event page lists all RSVPs so you know exactly who to expect. Attendees can see each other too.

Tip: Post an update a day before your event as a friendly reminder — attendance goes up!
